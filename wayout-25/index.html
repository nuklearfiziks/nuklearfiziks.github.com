The Way Out Club 25th Anniversary Extravaganza photos!

August 28, 2018

Every Saturday the last few months, I’ve had the immense pleasure of being able to shoot photos at The Way Out Club, one of London’s longest-running transgender club nights.

This weekend marked the club’s 25th anniversary and I took nearly 500 photos. Full gallery at nuklear.photos.
